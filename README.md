📊 Power BI Sales Performance Assessment Dashboard
📌 Overview
This project presents a comprehensive Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI. It focuses on delivering actionable insights through executive-level summaries and detailed analytical views of sales, profit, growth trends, and brand performance.
The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders understand key business drivers and make data-driven decisions.
________________________________________
🎯 Objectives
•	Analyze sales distribution across countries and distributors
•	Evaluate sales representative performance
•	Track Year-over-Year (YoY) growth trends
•	Identify top-performing regions and brands
•	Analyze profitability and inventory costs
•	Forecast future sales trends
________________________________________
📊 Dashboard Sections
1️⃣ Executive Overview
Provides high-level KPIs and performance indicators:
•	Sales by Country (Colombia leading revenue)
•	Sales by Distributor (Tottus dominates channel)
•	Sales Representative Performance (Movistar leading)
•	Top 5 Countries contribute ~85% of total revenue
________________________________________
2️⃣ Growth Analysis
•	YoY Growth trends with major spike in 2021 (~130%)
•	Sales dip in 2020 (~-24.5%) indicating market volatility
•	Seasonal trends and time-series analysis
________________________________________
3️⃣ Cost & Profit Analysis
•	Ireland has highest inventory cost (~$18M) with strong profit contribution
•	Net profit increased over 170% from 2018 to 2022
•	Colombia identified as the most profitable region
________________________________________
4️⃣ Brand Performance
•	Apple ($32.1M) and LG ($31.8M) dominate market share (>45%)
•	Significant growth observed across all brands in 2021
•	Comparative yearly brand analysis
________________________________________
5️⃣ Sales Forecasting
•	Cyclical sales behavior identified (peaks and drops)
•	Recovery trend observed in recent months
•	Positive outlook for future sales growth
________________________________________
📈 Key Insights
•	📌 Strong business performance with 127M total sales & 35% profit margin
•	🌍 Colombia and Tottus are primary revenue drivers
•	📱 Apple is the leading product category
•	📊 Growth volatility highlights need for stabilization strategies
•	📉 2020 downturn followed by strong recovery
